Who We Are

Elevate Legal Nurse Consulting is a team of healthcare experts assisting attorneys and their legal teams strategize, and execute medically focused cases. ELNC supports your litigation strategy by translating medically focused facts into digestible, case supporting content.

Mission

Elevate Legal Nurse Consulting continues to elevate the role of the nurse as consultant, interdisciplinary team member, and advocate for quality patient care. Our mission includes staying current with the ever-evolving clinical guidelines and ethics driving healthcare and patient care. ELNC and its team of healthcare experts revere the standard of care and strive to ensure patients’ rights are upheld through their legal support services.

Why work with Elevate Legal Nurse Consulting?

As healthcare professionals we hold an unmatched set of skills and seek to collaborate as a member of your legal team. We are your partner in practice, providing services (link to services page) to help bridge the gap between medicine and law. With ELNC, it’s personal. You are not just another client, and we are more than a third party vendor.
