Who We Are

Elevate Legal Nurse Consulting (ELNC) is a dedicated team of healthcare experts who specialize in providing tailored medicolegal services to attorney clients and their legal team. With our headquarters in Salt Lake City, Utah we serve both plaintiff and defense clients across the United States.

Mission

Elevate Legal Nurse Consulting continues to elevate the role of the nurse as consultant, interdisciplinary team member, and advocate for quality patient care. Our mission includes staying current with the ever-evolving clinical guidelines and ethics driving healthcare and patient care. ELNC and its team of healthcare experts revere the standard of care and strive to ensure patients’ rights are upheld through their legal support services.

Why work with Elevate Legal Nurse Consulting?

As healthcare professionals we hold an unmatched set of skills and collaborate as an extension of your legal team. We are your partner in practice, providing services to help bridge the gap between medicine and law. With ELNC, it’s personal. You are not just another client, and we are more than a third-party vendor.

Vision

We are partners in practice, and a stand for improving the healthcare system through generating opportunities for conversation and change from medicolegal case outcomes.

Our vision at Elevate Legal Nurse Consulting is to be the preferred partner and resource for driving change in healthcare. To err is human and mistakes will happen. It is a greater miss to not transform an oversight into an opportunity. By pairing healthcare knowledge with the influence of the law, we can generate opportunities for conversation and change within the healthcare system.
